## The Challenge
In 2023, Polaris Dentistry was a small-time practice struggling to bring in enough clients to stay open. The owner, Shilpa, faced a critical crossroads: she needed to revolutionize her marketing tactics quickly but had a limited budget that afforded zero room for wasteful spending on campaigns that didn't deliver immediate results.

## The Creekside Solution
We transformed a previously ineffective advertising approach into a high-ROI engine by applying advanced campaign management skills:
* **High-Intent Targeting:** We focused exclusively on high purchase intent local keywords to drive direct traffic to the office.
* **Technical Optimization:** Leveraged specialized tools within Google Ads to accelerate account optimization speed.
* **Creative Copywriting:** Crafted compelling, professional ad copy designed specifically to generate high Click-Through Rates (CTRs).

## The Results: The "Creekside" Turnaround
The difference in performance before and after our intervention was immediate and sustainable.

| Metric | Before Creekside (1 Year) | After Creekside (1 Year) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Conversions** | 26.00 | 413.00 |
| **Clicks** | 358 | 1.69K |
| **Cost Per Conv.** | $48.79 | $9.58 |
| **Total ROI** | Ineffective | Over 500% |

Nearly a year into our management, we continued to drive performance even lower, eventually reaching a cost-per-conversion of **below $4**. This expertise added thousands of dollars in additional revenue to their bottom line every single month.
