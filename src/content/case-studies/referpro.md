## The Challenge
In late 2024, Murphy & ReferPro raised $2.7 million in seed funding for their referral automation software aimed at home service contractors. While they had a proven product and offer, they lacked a consistent, scalable system for generating inbound leads. Their target market—contractors—often didn't even know such a solution existed, making traditional search-only strategies ineffective.

## The Creekside Solution
Creekside Marketing Pros implemented a "surround sound" digital strategy designed to educate the market and then capture the resulting interest:
* **Top-of-Funnel Awareness (Meta):** Launched Meta Ads to introduce the solution to business owners, moving decision-makers from Top-of-Funnel (TOF) to Bottom-of-Funnel (BOF).
* **Demand Capture (Google Ads):** Used Google Ads to capture the specific search intent generated by the Meta awareness campaigns.
* **The 'Metazation' Bridge:** After three months of initial growth, we leveraged a proprietary "Metazation" strategy using **Performance Max** to replicate Meta's audience demand generation directly inside Google.

## The Results: Repeatable B2B Growth
Between February 1, 2025, and July 27, 2025, ReferPro achieved a complete transformation of its inbound pipeline.

### Performance Breakdown:
* **ARR Growth:** Successfully **doubled their Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)** within just six months of the first seed round.
* **Lead Volume:** Doubled inbound lead flow by creating a repeatable system where Meta generates demand and Google captures it.
* **Scalability:** The efficiency of the initial 3-month run allowed ReferPro to **double their monthly ad spend** while maintaining high performance.
* **Campaign Reach:** Achieved over **542K impressions** and **299 conversions** during the primary scaling period.

By synchronizing social awareness with search intent, we created a repeatable growth engine that transformed ReferPro from a newly funded startup into a market-scaling leader.
